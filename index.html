The Allen Family

The main purpose of this page is to provide genealogical information of our family and provide links to help people in their research.

Allen

The Allen family came from Chester, South Carolina in the early 1800's, passing through Georgia and settling in Mississippi. The Eaton family came through Georgia about 1840 and settled in Alabama. The Haynes family had similar migration patterns from Virginia, Georgia, and then to Mississippi.

Brockway

The Brockway family came from England in 1638 to settle in Lyme, Connecticut. From there the migration across the US brought our branch of the family to Illinois, Montana, Idaho, Oregon, and California.

Marx

The Marx family came from Palanka, Austria Hungary through New York 1905-7, finally settling in California. The Wagner and Miesse family history takes us to Berks County, Pennsylvania, with connections to Alsace Lorraine.

Lemire

The Lemire family came from Quebec with ancestry that migrated from France in the 1600's. The Woodford family comes from Australia coming from England in the 1800's and then to Utah.
